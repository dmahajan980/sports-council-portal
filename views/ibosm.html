The BITS Open Sports Meet(BOSM) is the annual sports festival of BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus. This much awaited sports extravaganza, held from 13th September to 17th September, is a platform for college teams to exhibit their exceptional sporting talents and compete for superiority.
